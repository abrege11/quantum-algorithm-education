Quantum Algorithm Education

Abraham Brege

QAE is a project I started in the latter half of the 2024 summer as I intend to pursue graduate education in quantum algorithm research.

I made this website as a resource for future employers, and graduate schools to see my understanding of these algorithms. After all, teaching is the best way to learn, not to mention that I would love to be a professor at some point in my career.

Now, while I do believe that the material on this website is effectively teaching these concepts, remember that these algorithm breakdowns are all interpretations of the material I have studied. While all information is factual, you can get a more in depth education on most these topics by studying something like Nielsen and Chuang's "Quantum Computation and Quantum Information".


Lastly, note that all material is 100% original. Obviously, I learned from a plethora of online resources, from the original research papers, to Qiskit's online resources, to random YouTube videos. However, all content was written by me. Likewise every image, circuit diagram, table, even the modal functionality, was created/implemented by me
